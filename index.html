Hansen Hollow

Why stay in a crowded condo and have a shared wall with your neighbors when you can stay in a luxurious home on over 3.25 acres in quiet privacy?

Hansen Hollow is located in Liberty, UT. Just minutes from 3 well known ski resorts: Powder Mountain, Wolf Mountain, and Snowbasin - home of the 2002 Winter Olympic Downhill and Super G events.

These luxury accommodations are perfect for a family reunion, vacation or romantic getaway. Just 60 minutes from downtown Salt Lake City. Liberty is close enough to Salt Lake City to offer convenience, while far enough to get you out town for a peaceful holiday. Enjoy world class skiing, snow boarding, sledding, snow shoeing, snowmobiling, Heli Skiing, ice skating, swimming in your own private indoor pool or soaking in the hot tub in the winter.
